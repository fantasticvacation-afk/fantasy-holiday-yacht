Risk Factors披露

When evaluating the Company's value, investors should thoroughly understand and carefully consider the following risk factors. Management continuously monitors and manages these risks, taking proactive mitigation measures.

⚠️

⚠️ Macroeconomic Cyclical Risk

豪华游艇消费与宏观经济环境和消费者信心密切相关。经济下行期高端消费支出可能缩减，影响Custom和charter business收入。公司通过业务多元化（Custom/Charter/Management/Membership四大板块）和经常性收入Share提升（Management+MembershipTotal33%）来缓冲周期性波动

⚠️

⚠️ Project Execution & Weather Risk

游艇custom projects从签约到交付周期较长（通常12-24个月），极端天气事件（台风、飓风等）可能对船队运营和航线安排造成影响。公司通过全球多区域布局和季节性route planning分散风险，2025因天气原因取消/改期's 航次仅占2.3%

⚠️

⚠️ Industry Competition Risk

International yacht groups continue to deepen their presence in China, and new entrants may introduce pricing competition. The Company maintains a premium positioning and value-competition strategy, with an industry-leading client NPS score of 82. We sustain competitive moats through expertise and service excellence — we compete on value, not price.

⚠️

⚠️ Regulatory Policy Risk

Operating across multiple jurisdictions involves various maritime regulations, environmental standards, and tax policies, where changes may affect operating costs and business models. The Company maintains a dedicated compliance team with multi-jurisdictional policy tracking mechanisms and partnerships with leading international maritime law firms.
