Milestones

A chronicle of Fantastic Vacation's key strategic milestones from founding to present

2010
Three veteran yachting professionals founded the 'Fantastic Vacation Studio' in Nanshan, Shenzhen. The initial team of 12, with registered capital of 5 million RMB, focused on yacht charter brokerage and small-scale custom consulting.

Established 'Global, Full-Chain' strategic DNA

2012
Partnered with a renowned Italian shipyard to deliver the first 50-foot fully custom luxury motor yacht 'Sea Melody', marking the company's official transformation from charter brokerage to a full-custom yacht service provider.

First custom yacht delivered → View Case

2015
Established the Asia-Pacific operations center in Singapore and obtained authorized partnership credentials with Ferretti Group, formally establishing strategic alliances with top-tier shipbuilding groups.

First international service center launched + strategic shipyard partnerships

2017
Managed fleet surpassed 20 vessels, including the first 80+ foot superyacht. Built an in-house Yacht Manager team and launched 24/7 global emergency response system.

Scaled management services, setting industry benchmarks

2020
Established the European operations center in Monaco (摩纳哥) and became a full member of the Mediterranean Marina Association. Global service network now covers three core markets: Mediterranean, Caribbean, and Southeast Asia.

European expansion complete, accelerating international growth

2023
Awarded 'Asia's Best Yacht Service Provider' by Asia Boating Awards (2021-2023, three consecutive years). Established technical partnerships with Feadship and Lürssen in the same year.

Asia's Best Yacht Service Provider (3 Consecutive Years) → View News

2024
Delivered the 82m superyacht 'Blue Mirage' featuring a helipad and submarine garage, winning Asia Boating Awards Custom Yacht of the Year. Officially achieved ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification.

Flagship project delivered + international quality certification

All service processes are certified by the International Organization for Standardization, ensuring full-chain quality control from initial consultation through after-sales maintenance. Every service you receive follows rigorously audited standard procedures.

Ferretti Group Authorized Partner
