📋 Project Background

The client, a leading Asia-Pacific multinational, planned its annual executive summit and team-building activities. They required a professional team to provide end-to-end maritime event management — encompassing yacht fleet formation sailing, business conference facilities, team-building activity design, and comprehensive safety management.

🎯 Core Requirements

  • 3 yachts in fleet formation, accommodating 200executives and guests
  • Large conference room equipped for video conferencing and keynote presentations
  • Team-building activity curation (water sports, shore excursions, themed evening events)
  • Michelin-starred chef brigade providing business banquet and buffet service
  • Full medical safety assurance with onboard doctors and emergency equipment
  • Corporate gala dinner planning and execution
