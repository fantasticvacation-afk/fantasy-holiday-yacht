📋 Project Background

The client, a prominent Asian family, planned an unprecedented at-sea wedding for their only daughter. The celebration needed to accommodate 120guests，7 days6 nights, with an itinerary spanning the Caribbean's most beautiful islands — seamlessly integrating Chinese wedding traditions with Western luxury.

🎯 Core Requirements

  • 85-meter-plus superyacht accommodating up to 120guests and45service staff

  • Underwater party space supporting snorkeling and underwater photography
  • Chinese wedding ceremony space (tea ceremony and bowing rites) alongside a Western banquet venue
  • Michelin-starred chef team providing Chinese wedding banquet and Western buffet
  • 120guests'7 days of accommodation and curated activities

core service highlights

Differentiated capabilities — Crafting a one-of-a-kind maritime wedding legend

🚁 helicopter aerial wedding

The bride made her entrance via helicopter, descending onto the 85-meter superyacht helideck landing. Supported throughout by a professional air traffic coordination team — a perfect fusion of safety and romance.

🎎 Chinese ceremony venue customization

A traditional Chinese wedding ceremony venue constructed on the yacht deck, complete with tea ceremony platform, bowing area, and double-happiness decorations. Faithfully honoring Chinese wedding traditions — a perfect marriage of maritime grandeur and cultural heritage.

🤿 underwater party space

A professional underwater party platform enabling guests to join the celebration via snorkeling or scuba diving. An underwater photography team captures every guest's spectacular underwater moments.

👨‍🍳 Michelin wedding banquet customization

A Michelin two-star chef brigade offering a dual-menu experience: traditional Chinese wedding banquet (Dragon & Phoenix, Fortune's Arrival, and more) and Western buffet. Supporting 120guests dining simultaneously。

📹 Full-event live broadcast and documentary

A professional photo and video team documenting every moment of the wedding. Live streaming enables remote participation by friends and family unable to attend. Final delivery includes 90-minute wedding documentary film.
