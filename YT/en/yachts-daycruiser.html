Day Cruiser Series

Sun, Sea & Sophistication — Luxury Day Boat Collection

Series Overview

The Day Cruiser Series is designed for those who pursue the purest form of on-water enjoyment — spontaneous, stylish, and exquisitely crafted. From the 20-meter Sport to the 32-meter Grand Day, each Day Cruiser combines high-performance hull dynamics, open-plan social deck layouts, and meticulous Italian detailing. Built for day escapes, sunset champagne cruises, and weekend coastal adventures, these vessels deliver maximum pleasure with minimal crew requirements.
