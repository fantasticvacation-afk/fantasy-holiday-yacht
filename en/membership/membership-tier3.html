Membership Tiers

Fantastic Vacation Platinum is the premium tier for yacht owners and high-net-worth individuals with frequent charter needs. Building on Gold Sail, Platinum expands global service coverage with 24/7 dedicated butler, premium berth guarantee, and complimentary yacht management consultations.

Exclusive Benefits

Global Exclusive Events (min. 12/year, including Europe/Caribbean special sessions)

Includes product launches, nautical salons, and annual member gatherings — plus Europe and Caribbean special sessions with deep networking opportunities in the elite yachting circle.

Premium Berth Guarantee (60-day advance + global priority access)

Enjoy 60-day advance priority booking at all partner marinas, with guaranteed berth access worldwide — including Monaco and St. Tropez.

Member-Exclusive Route Pricing (15% off + complimentary vessel upgrade)

Platinum Members enjoy exclusive 15% off all Fantastic Vacation curated routes, plus complimentary vessel upgrades (subject to availability).

Monthly Voyage Report + Dedicated Itinerary Planning Team

Monthly voyage activity reports with a dedicated itinerary planning team providing one-on-one route design and shoreside activity arrangements.

24/7 Dedicated Butler (multilingual + global time zone coverage)

Platinum Members receive a dedicated butler team, responding 24/7 in Chinese, English, French, and Italian — covering all global time zones.

Complimentary Yacht Management Consultation (valued at 30,000/year)

Enjoy complimentary professional yacht management consultation valued at 30,000/year, covering maintenance planning, crew management, and insurance advisory.

Invitation to the "Fantasy Club" Elite Social Circle

Join the Fantasy Club elite circle — connect with global yacht enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, and investors to expand your premium network.

Who It's For

Platinum is suited for: high-net-worth individuals with private yachts or frequent charter needs; entrepreneurs and executives seeking full-spectrum butler service; investors and social elites looking to expand their network within the yachting circle.

Application Notes

  • Eligibility — Age 18+, financial qualification verified, no adverse credit record. Silver Sail and Gold Sail members are welcome to apply for upgrade
  • Required Documents — Proof of identity, contact information, financial qualification (bank statements or asset proof)
  • Review Timeline — Complete application review within 5-10 business days
  • Annual Fee Payment — Pay the annual fee upon approval to activate all membership benefits
