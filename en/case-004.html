📋 Project Background

The client, a renowned environmental entrepreneur, has long championed climate change awareness and polar ecosystem conservation. The brief called for a professional expedition yacht capable of both personal polar exploration and supporting scientific research teams at sea.

🎯 Core Requirements

  • Ice-class hull certification enabling safe navigation deep into Antarctic and Alaskan waters
  • Solar hybrid propulsion system minimizing the carbon footprint
  • range required to reach8,000 nautical miles supporting extended scientific expeditions
  • Professional scientific equipment interfaces supporting underwater sampling and data acquisition
  • cabins can accommodate12guests and8research personnel
  • Satellite communications system enabling polar remote data transmission
  • Helicopter deck supporting ice-based scientific operations and emergency rescue

⚠️ Project Challenges

  • Technical integration of ice-class hull structure with eco-friendly propulsion systems
  • Equipment reliability and crew safety in extreme polar low-temperature environments
  • Standardized interface design for scientific equipment integration with the yacht platform
  • Antarctic and Alaskan route regulatory compliance clearance
  • Risk management and emergency response planning for polar scientific expeditions
