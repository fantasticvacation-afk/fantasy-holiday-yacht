Management's Discussion & Analysis

以下为管理层对2025度经营情况's 回顾与2026战略展望，旨在帮助投资者全面理解公司经营成果、财务状况及未来发展方向。

2025度经营回顾

Custom业务里程碑：2025completedcustom yachts delivered5艘，总合同金额约1.83亿元，客户满意度4.8/5.0。重点交付项目包括首艘50米以上超级游艇Feadship Pure 55m，标志着公司custom capabilities进入国际一流行列。在手订单7艘约2.3亿元，为2026-2027年业绩provide坚实支撑。

charter business增长：416 charters completed (up 16% YoY), serving 1,280 clients. The Mediterranean remains the most popular route (32%), with the Caribbean (25%) and Southeast Asia (18%) sustaining rapid growth. Following the opening of the Miami hub, Americas route revenue grew 156% YoY, emerging as a new growth pillar.

Management服务深化：在管船队突破52艘，新增8份management contracts含3艘100英尺+超级游艇。renewal rate91%创新高，客户NPS 82分。推出智能船队管理平台，提升运维效率15%。

Membership体系Upgrade：五级Membership体系运行首个完整年度，Membership达687位（+25%），Equity使用率从52%提升至73%。新推出全球泊位通、航海学院等5项专属服务，Black Card member平均年消费超500万元。

2026战略展望

全球化深化：Plans for 2026 include launching a Bangkok regional service point to complete initial Southeast Asian market presence. Investment of approximately RMB 80 million (2026-2027) in the Shenzhen headquarters digital operations platform upgrade to comprehensively enhance client service and operational efficiency.

绿色转型推进：启动ISO 14064温室气体核查认证，推进马尔代夫海草床保护等新碳汇项目。与船厂联合推动绿色动力方案，目标2028年新custom projects全部provide绿色动力选项。

财务展望：Based on 7 vessels on order valued at approximately RMB 230 million, an 87% membership renewal rate, and global economic conditions, management remains optimistic about 2026 performance, forecasting full-year revenue growth above 30%, managed fleet expansion to 65+ vessels, and total membership approaching 900.
