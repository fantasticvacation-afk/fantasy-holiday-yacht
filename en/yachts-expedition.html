Expedition Series

Fear No Frontier — Long-Range Explorer Yacht Collection

Series Overview

The Expedition Series is designed for owners passionate about remote ocean exploration and spiritual discovery. From the 50-meter Ocean Edition to the 70-meter Explorer, every Expedition yacht features ice-strengthened hulls, extended fuel tank systems, and all-weather stability design, capable of safe navigation in extreme environments such as Antarctica, the Arctic, and uninhabited Pacific waters — while maintaining breathtaking luxury and comfort. Our Expedition Series empowers you to explore the world's most mysterious waters without compromise.
