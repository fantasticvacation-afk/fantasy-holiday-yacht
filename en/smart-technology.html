Smart Technology

FANTASTIC VACATION exterior yacht design — partnering with world-leading naval architecture studios to craft iconic silhouettes from hull lines to superstructure.

🤖

Exterior Design — Defining the Visual Language of the Sea

"A great yacht is recognizable from a hundred meters away. Its silhouette, posture, and relationship with the ocean are all unique aesthetic declarations."

Design Philosophy

Smart technology is the neural network of a yacht. At Fantastic Vacation, we abandon the "pile on equipment" mindset for a "scenario-driven" integration methodology — not asking "what can we install" but "what experience does the owner need at sea."

Our smart tech team includes former Tesla autonomous driving engineers, aerospace communications specialists, and professional AV system designers. "Zero learning curve" is our design principle — every smart system should be as intuitive as a smartphone. From central control touchscreens to voice assistants, from remote phone monitoring to AI auto-scene switching, we create not just technology, but an ambient intelligent lifestyle.

All systems use modular architecture supporting 5–10 years of future hardware and software upgrades. Security follows IEC 62443 industrial control cybersecurity standards, protecting yacht systems from cyber threats.

Core Technical Dimensions

🖥️ Central Automation

Crestron/Lutron integrated vessel automation via salon touchscreen, cabin panels, and iPad/phone app — controlling lighting, curtains, HVAC, AV, and security. One-touch scene presets: "Sunrise Wake", "Party Mode", "Anchored Night", "Away Security". AI learns owner habits, auto-optimizing temperature and lighting for 15–20% energy savings.

📡 Satellite Communications

Starlink Maritime + KVH TracPhone V30 dual-satellite redundant links delivering high-speed internet globally (up to 350Mbps downlink). VSAT supports 4K video conferencing, streaming, and telemedicine. Iridium Certus backup ensures basic communications in extreme weather or high latitudes. Vessel-wide Wi-Fi 6 with seamless roaming.

🎬 AV Entertainment

IMAX-grade private cinema (7.2.4 Dolby Atmos, 4K laser projection, 150-inch motorized screen). Multi-zone distributed audio (Bose/B&W/Meridian) across salon, flybridge, and beach club. Outdoor waterproof TVs (SunBriteTV) with concealed lift projectors. AirPlay/Chromecast throughout, independent audio per cabin. Optional karaoke with multilingual library.

🔒 Security & Surveillance

Full-vessel HD CCTV (IR night vision) covering all entries, aft deck, engine room, and surrounding waters. AI person detection with intrusion alerts pushed to owner's phone. Underwater sonar detection against diver intrusion. Electronic fence creates 360-degree virtual perimeter at anchor. Integrated with Sea-Fire automatic fire suppression and gas detection sensors for comprehensive active safety.
