Sovereign Series

Master the Seas — Flagship Superyacht Collection

Series Overview

The Sovereign Series represents the pinnacle of Fantastic Vacation yacht engineering, crafted exclusively for the world's elite families, royalty, and ultra-high-net-worth individuals. From the 78-meter flagship to the 120-meter Legend-class, every Sovereign yacht is a fusion of Italian design and Dutch engineering excellence. We collaborate with the world's top shipyards, integrating cutting-edge marine technology with extraordinary luxury to deliver an unparalleled ocean living experience for discerning owners.
