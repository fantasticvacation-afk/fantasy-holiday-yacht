200+ HNW Clients

6 Global Hubs

Triple ISO Certified

Core Business Segments

🛥️ Yacht Customization & Brokerage

Representing world-class shipyards including Ferretti Group (Italy), Feadship (Netherlands), and Lürssen (Germany), delivering end-to-end custom solutions — from requirements analysis, concept design, interior & exterior customization, construction supervision, sea trials, delivery training, to after-sales assurance. 23 custom yachts delivered, with a record single-vessel value of RMB 280 million

🌊 Charter Route Operations

Covering 20+ premier cruising grounds worldwide — Mediterranean, Caribbean, Southeast Asia, South Pacific, and Nordic Fjords. Fleet of 50+ owned and partnered yachts offering half-day experiences, full-day charters, multi-day cruises, and themed custom itineraries. 380+ charter voyages completed in 2025

⚙️ Management & Maintenance

360° full-lifecycle yacht management: daily operations (crew management, supply scheduling), scheduled maintenance (hull cleaning, mechanical inspection), professional repairs (engine overhaul, electronic system upgrades), maritime compliance (surveys, insurance, registration), and asset value preservation planning. Total fleet value under management exceeds RMB 4.5 billion

👑 Premium Membership Program

Five-tier membership — Silver Sail / Gold Sail / Platinum / Diamond / Black Card — granting 150+ exclusive privileges including global berth reservations, elite networking events, RYA-certified nautical training, dedicated concierge service, and helicopter transfers. Average annual member spending exceeds RMB 1.2 million
