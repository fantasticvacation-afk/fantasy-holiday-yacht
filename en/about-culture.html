✦ OUR CULTURE ✦

Culture

以海洋为信仰，以匠心为传承

我们的核心价值观

Fantastic Vacation的Culture根植于对海洋的敬畏与对工艺的极致追求

⚓

匠人之心

我们相信每一艘游艇都是独一无二的艺术品。从概念到交付，数百道工序须经资深工程师逐一审核，绝不妥协任何细节。我们的质量标准参照国际高端船级社规范，确保每一处焊缝、每一寸内饰都达到收藏级水准。

🌊

海洋信仰

海洋不仅是我们的工作场景，更是灵感之源与精神归属。我们倡导负责任的海洋生活理念，在船队运营、航线规划、废弃物管理中全面践行环保标准，致力于保护这颗蓝色星球的每一片海域。

🤝

客⼾共⽣

我们不以「供应商」自居，而是每一位船东的长期合作伙伴。从第一次需求访谈开始，我们便以船东的视角思考每一个决策——航线是否舒适、设计是否人性化、维保是否省心。您的满意是衡量我们成功的核心标准。

🌍

全球视野

我们的团队成员来自全球多个国家与地区，涵盖游艇设计、船舶工程、酒店管理、金融法律等多元背景。这种跨界融合带来了更开阔的行业视野，也确保我们能够以国际标准服务每一位来自世界各地的船东。

我们的工作方式

一套经过十五年打磨的协作体系，确保每一个项目都精确运行

1

深度倾听

每次合作都始于深入理解您的需求、生活方式与海洋愿景

2

跨部门协同

设计、工程、项目管理、法务、财务多团队并行推进

3

透明沟通

定期进度报告、现场照片、视频会议，您可实时掌握项目进展

4

终身服务

项目交付不是终点——我们的管家服务将持续陪伴您与游艇的每一段航程

Our Mission

To deliver extraordinary maritime lifestyle experiences through world-class yacht services, connecting discerning clients with the freedom of the world's most beautiful waters.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative luxury yacht integrated service brand globally, setting the benchmark for excellence in customization, charter, management, and membership services by 2030.

Our Core Values

🎯 Excellence First

We pursue perfection in every detail — from yacht design to guest experience, from maintenance protocols to client communication. Excellence is not an aspiration; it is our minimum standard.

🤝 Integrity & Trust

We build lasting relationships through transparency, honesty, and accountability. Every commitment made is a commitment kept — this is the foundation of our 65%+ repeat client rate.

💡 Innovation Driven

We embrace cutting-edge technology — from electric propulsion systems to AI-powered maintenance diagnostics and digital client platforms — continuously raising the bar for the yachting industry.

🌍 Global Perspective

Operating across 6 global hubs with a multicultural team of 200+ professionals, we deliver locally relevant, globally informed solutions that transcend borders and cultures.

🌱 Sustainability

We are committed to reducing carbon intensity by 40% by 2030. Our green fleet share exceeds 30%, and we integrate environmental stewardship into every operational decision.

❤️ Client-Centric

Every client journey is unique. We listen, anticipate, and craft bespoke solutions — whether it is a custom yacht build or a once-in-a-lifetime charter experience — with uncompromising attention to personal preferences.

Our Code of Conduct

Guiding principles that define how we operate and interact

1. Safety Always Comes First

Zero compromise on safety for crew, guests, and the environment — every vessel, every voyage, every time.

2. Respect for People & Planet

We treat every colleague, client, and partner with dignity while minimizing our ecological footprint.

3. Continuous Learning

We invest in our people — RYA certifications, technical workshops, leadership programs — building expertise for the long term.

4. Collaborative Success

We believe the best outcomes emerge when diverse expertise converges — designers, engineers, captains, and clients working as one team.

5. Accountability & Ownership

Every team member takes ownership of results. We celebrate wins and learn from setbacks with equal candor.

6. Discretion & Privacy

Our clients' privacy is sacred. All interactions, transactions, and personal data are handled with the utmost confidentiality.
