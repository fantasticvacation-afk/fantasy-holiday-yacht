Management Service Process

Six-step standardized process — from contract signing to delivery, fully transparent and manageable

01

Asset Assessment & Needs Analysis

Comprehensive inspection of the yacht's current condition, including age, equipment status, certificate validity, and historical maintenance records, to develop a personalized management plan tailored to the owner's preferences.

02

Custom Management Plan Design

Based on the assessment, we design a tailored management plan covering crew allocation, maintenance schedules, budget planning, and route planning, with clearly defined service scope and KPIs.

03

Professional Team Onboarding & Handover

Licensed captains, chief engineers, and service teams complete a full handover, establishing a digital archive system and activating the owner's exclusive app for real-time monitoring.

04

Routine Operations & Maintenance Execution

Scheduled daily operations, periodic inspections, monthly financial reports, and quarterly safety audits. All key milestones require owner approval before proceeding.

05

Annual Comprehensive Assessment Report

A comprehensive Yacht Asset Health Report is issued annually, including technical condition ratings, market valuation changes, optimization recommendations, and budget planning for the upcoming year.

06

Continuous Optimization & Value-Added Services

Based on usage data andIndustry Trends, proactively proposing value-added solutions such as refits, route expansion, and shared chartering to continually enhance asset value.
