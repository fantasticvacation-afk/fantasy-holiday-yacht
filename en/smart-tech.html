Smart Technology — The Power of the Invisible

"The highest form of technology is that which goes unnoticed—yet makes everything better."

Design Philosophy

Smart technology is the "fifth dimension" of modern superyachts—after length, beam, height, and time, technology endows a yacht with the ability to perceive, think, and respond. Fantastic Vacation's smart systems team comprises senior engineers from avionics, IoT, and cybersecurity backgrounds. Our mission is to elegantly integrate military-grade and aerospace-grade intelligence into a floating home.

Our smart technology solutions follow the "Quiet Intelligence" principle—all technical computation runs silently in the background, user interfaces are radically simple, and response times are measured in milliseconds. Over 2,000 sensor nodes are deployed throughout the vessel, continuously collecting and analyzing hull attitude, environmental parameters, system status, and energy consumption data. Through redundant ring network architecture and edge computing nodes, all smart systems maintain full autonomous operation even during satellite communication outages.

Core Technology Systems

🧠 Integrated Vessel Environment System (IVES)

Core Architecture: Redundant computing cluster based on Intel Xeon processors + FPGA co-processors, running our proprietary IVES platform. Through 2,000+ sensors and 200+ actuators, achieves unified monitoring and control of all 58 vessel subsystems.
User Interface: Multiple high-resolution touch control panels throughout (27" 4K in main salon, 15" in guest cabins, 10" in crew areas), with wireless iPad Pro and iPhone control. UI follows the "three-layer rule": daily operations in one tap, advanced settings within two levels, engineering mode requires biometric verification.
Smart Scenes: 12 one-touch presets including "Cruise Mode," "At Anchor Mode," "Sleep Mode," "Party Mode," "Away Mode," and "Security Mode." Each scene simultaneously adjusts lighting, HVAC, curtains, audio, surveillance, water supply, and energy distribution across 20+ subsystems.

🛰️ Communications & Connectivity

Satellite Communications: Dual-antenna VSAT (KVH TracPhone V30, C/Ku/Ka multi-band) + Starlink Maritime (up to 220 Mbps download) + Iridium Certus LEO backup, creating three-layer satellite redundancy. 4G/5G near-shore access with automatic seamless optimal-link switching. Wi-Fi 6E coverage throughout (including flybridge and beach club), supporting 150+ simultaneous devices, 4K video conferencing, real-time telemedicine, and cloud collaboration.
Internal Communications: Vessel-wide VoIP voice system + digital intercom (engine room, bridge, galley, crew areas) + cordless telephone subsystem. Multi-language real-time translation (built-in AI engine, supporting 12 languages including Chinese, English, French, Italian, Spanish, Japanese, Arabic).

🎬 Entertainment & AV Systems

Cinema System: Main salon or dedicated cinema room with IMAX Enhanced 4K laser projector (Sony or Barco professional-grade) + Dolby Atmos 11.2.4 immersive audio (Bowers & Wilkins or Focal Utopia series). Screen size 8–15 feet, Dolby Vision HDR support. Professional acoustic treatment achieves 0.3–0.6 second reverb time.
Whole-Home Audio: Zonal background music system (Sonance/Bang & Olufsen) supporting AirPlay 2, Spotify Connect, Tidal Masters, and local NAS lossless library. Independent source selection and volume per zone, with "music follow" feature (audio seamlessly transitions to your current zone).
Outdoor Entertainment: Waterproof audio on flybridge/sun deck, underwater speakers (submersed music for swimmers), deck LED screen (movies, sports, DJ visualizations).

💡 Lighting & Environmental Intelligence

Vessel-Wide LED Lighting: 500+ independently controllable LED fixtures and strips, color temperature 2,000K–6,500K stepless, 16 million colors. Four layers: ambient, task, accent, and decorative. All cabin lighting scenes synchronized with time—gradual warm wake-up at sunrise, neutral-bright midday, warm-dim evening, amber sleep aid at night.
Underwater Lighting: Multiple RGBW underwater LED units at stern (OceanLED or Lumishore), single unit output up to 30,000+ lumens. Effects include static color, breathing gradient, music beat sync, bioluminescent attraction mode (attracting plankton for natural light show). Color and intensity adjustable via IVES or mobile.
Smart Curtains: Vessel-wide motorized curtain system (Lutron or Somfy), supporting segmented control, timed scenes, sunlight-linked (auto-close on strong sun), and away mode (all closed). All motors silent (≤35 dB), fabrics with UV-blocking and thermal insulation.

🔒 Security & Surveillance

Video Surveillance: 4K infrared night-vision cameras (with ANPR/vessel name auto-recognition) covering all access points, deck areas, engine room, and tender garage. AI-powered anomaly detection (unknown vessel approach, man overboard, smoke/flame recognition), alert response ≤2 seconds. All footage AES-256 encrypted, stored locally with optional satellite cloud backup.
Cybersecurity: Multi-layer firewall + intrusion detection system (IDS) + network segmentation (guest, vessel control, and crew networks physically separated), certified to ISO 27001 standards. Quarterly penetration testing by third-party white-hat teams.
Life Safety: Networked smoke/heat/CO detectors linked to fire pumps, fire doors, and ventilation shutdown. Man Overboard (MOB) auto-detection using thermal imaging + radar + AI video analysis triple confirmation, false alarm rate ≤0.01/year. Emergency lighting restores all-vessel illumination within 90 seconds of main power failure.
