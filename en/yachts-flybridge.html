Flybridge Series

Mediterranean Dream, Elegant Cruising — Classic Flybridge Yacht Series

Series Overview

The Flybridge Series is a versatile lineup that balances family vacations, daily cruising, and business entertainment. The elevated flybridge deck offers panoramic views and naturally extends social spaces outward — the main deck salon, flybridge alfresco dining area, and foredeck sunbathing zone work together to create multilayered seafaring lifestyles. Whether it's a multi-generational family holiday or maritime business hospitality, the Flybridge Series strikes an ideal balance between comfort and practicality.

Models offer flexible configurations across deck levels (two/three-tier flybridge), galley placement (upper/lower deck), and crew quarters layout, allowing owners to select based on their cruising preferences.
