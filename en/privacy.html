Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information

Our Commitment

隐私承诺

Fantastic Vacation Industrial Co., Ltd. values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. We strictly comply with the Personal Information Protection Law and related privacy regulations.

奇幻假期实业有限公司重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们在您使用网站和服务时如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的个人信息。我们严格遵守《个人信息保护法》及相关隐私法规。

Information We Collect

我们收集的信息

When you use our services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Information You Provide — Name, contact details, address, booking preferences, payment information, and membership application data
    您主动提供的信息 — 姓名、联系方式、地址、预订偏好、支付信息和会员申请数据
  • Automatically Collected Information — Browser type, IP address, access time, page view history, and device information
    自动收集的信息 — 浏览器类型、IP地址、访问时间、页面浏览记录和设备信息
  • Service-Generated Information — Yacht customization plans, charter records, management contracts, and customer communication content
    服务过程中产生的信息 — 游艇定制方案、租赁记录、托管合同和客户沟通内容
  • Sensitive Personal Information — We do not proactively collect sensitive information unless necessary for service delivery and with your explicit consent
    敏感个人信息 — 我们不会主动收集敏感信息，除非为服务必需且经您明确同意

How We Use & Protect Your Information

信息使用与保护

The information we collect is used to: provide and improve our services; process orders and communicate with customers; meet compliance requirements; and manage marketing and customer relationships. We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures — including SSL encrypted storage, firewall protection, and access control — to safeguard your data.

我们收集的信息用于：提供和改善服务；处理订单与客户沟通；合规需求保障；营销与客户关系管理。我们采用行业标准的加密和安全措施（包括SSL加密存储、防火墙保护和访问权限控制）保护您的数据安全。

Your Rights

您的权利

Under applicable laws, you enjoy the following privacy rights:

  • Right to Know — Understand how we collect and use your personal information
    知情权 — 了解我们如何收集和使用您的个人信息
  • Right to Access — Review the personal information we hold about you at any time
    访问权 — 随时查阅我们持有的关于您的个人信息
  • Right to Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
    更正权 — 要求更正不准确或不完整的个人信息
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your personal information under specific circumstances
    删除权 — 要求在特定情况下删除您的个人信息
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Withdraw your consent to personal information processing at any time
    撤回同意权 — 随时撤回对个人信息处理的同意
  • Right to Complain — File a complaint with the personal information protection regulatory authority
    投诉权 — 向个人信息保护监管机构提出投诉的权利
