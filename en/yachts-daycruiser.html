Day Cruiser Series

Boundless Days, Ultimate Freedom — Modern Day Cruiser Series

Series Overview

The Day Cruiser Series is designed for the next generation of seafaring lifestyles — Fantastic Vacation's entry-level luxury offering. Spanning a nimble 12m to 18m size range, these boats are purpose-built for high-speed day cruising, weekend parties, and short getaways. Design inspiration draws from the fluid aesthetics of the Italian Riviera — low sheer lines, open deck layouts, and minimalist interiors, infusing the fluidity of the ocean into every hull. While positioned as day cruisers, quality and service are never compromised — every model in the series enjoys the same concierge-level after-sales service and berthing guarantee as our flagship lines.

The Day Cruiser Series is perfectly suited for coastal-city owners, first-time yacht buyers, and those who value social and urban-surround cruising experiences. Whether it's an evening seaside cocktail party, a weekend family picnic at sea, or a one-day excursion in a city bay, every Day Cruiser model balances generous deck social space with private relaxation areas — all within a compact footprint.

For clients considering the transition from day cruising to larger yacht ownership, the Day Cruiser Series is also an ideal stepping stone — allowing you to familiarize yourself with the rhythm of nautical life and daily maintenance routines within a controlled budget.
