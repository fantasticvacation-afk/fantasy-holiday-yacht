FAQ

Are artworks safe on a yacht? ▼
We employ professional marine-grade artwork securing and climate control systems, providing comprehensive protection against yacht-specific challenges such as vibration, humidity, and salt spray, all covered by specialized insurance.
How long is the art customization cycle? ▼
Depending on the artist's schedule and artwork complexity, typically 3-8 months. We recommend initiating the art concept planning during the yacht construction phase to ensure seamless integration of space and artwork.
Can I participate in auctions? ▼
Available。Fantastic VacationMembers enjoy priority previews, proxy bidding services, and exclusive access to major global auction houses VIPSeats。
